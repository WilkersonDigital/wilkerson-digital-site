Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Overview

This policy explains how Wilkerson Digital LLC handles information when you use Emergency 18 or contact us. This initial policy is a practical starting point and should be reviewed before the app's public store release.

Information you provide

Depending on the features you use, you may provide account information, profile information, golf scores, round details, friend connections, support messages, bug reports, or feature requests.

Location information

Emergency 18 may request device location to support nearby-course discovery, course mapping, yardage tools, or other location-based features. Location access depends on your device permissions.

How information is used

  • Provide, maintain, and improve app features.
  • Save and synchronize rounds and social features.
  • Respond to support requests and investigate problems.
  • Protect the service from abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.

Service providers

We may use infrastructure and service providers for authentication, databases, mapping, course information, analytics, crash reporting, weather, and app distribution. These providers process information according to their own terms and privacy practices.

Data retention and deletion

Information may be retained while your account is active and as reasonably necessary to operate, secure, and comply with legal obligations. Account and data deletion instructions will be added before public release.

Children

Emergency 18 is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated as Emergency 18 changes. The revised date at the top will reflect the latest version.

Contact

Use the support page to contact Wilkerson Digital about privacy questions.
