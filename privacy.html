Privacy Policy

This policy explains what Emergency 18 may collect, why it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information you provide

Emergency 18 may process account and profile details, golf scores, round data, friend connections, golf bag information, support messages, bug reports, and feature requests.

Location

If you grant permission, Emergency 18 may use device location for nearby-course discovery, course maps, and yardage tools. You can change location access in your device settings.

How information is used

  • Operate, save, and synchronize app features.
  • Support live rounds and social features you choose to use.
  • Respond to support requests and improve reliability.
  • Protect accounts and the service from misuse.

Service providers

We use trusted providers for services such as account authentication, data storage, mapping, course information, and app distribution. Their handling of information is governed by their own terms and privacy practices.

Retention and deletion

Information may be kept while your account is active and as reasonably necessary to provide, secure, and maintain the service. To request account or data deletion, email support@wilkersondigital.net.

Children

Emergency 18 is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy as Emergency 18 changes. The date above will show the latest revision. Questions can be sent to support@wilkersondigital.net.
