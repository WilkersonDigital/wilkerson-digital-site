Support

Found a bug, have an account issue, or want to share feedback? Wilkerson Digital wants to hear it.

Before contacting support

  • Confirm you are using the newest available beta build.
  • Restart the app and try the action again.
  • Include the device model, iOS version, and what happened.
  • For round problems, mention the course and game format.
